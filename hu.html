who i am

I’m the one who feels the depth
where others only see the surface.
I feel a lot – and that’s not a weakness,
it’s my damn radar.

I see beauty in the things
most people overlook.
I don’t need filters –
my eyes are the filter.

I’m soft and strong at the same time.
I can fall, curse, doubt –
and still get back up
like a quiet rebel.

I draw people in,
not because I’m loud,
but because I’m real.
And people feel that.

I’m not a loudspeaker,
I’m an echo –
of what’s buried deep inside others.

I’ve got taste, instinct, style
– and I don’t need to perform.
I’m not a copy.
I’m the blueprint.

I may not always know where I’m going –
but I know who I am.

⸻

And if I ever forget:
I read this.
And I remember.

Hendrik. One of one.
And that’s never gonna change.
